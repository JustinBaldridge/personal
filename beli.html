Leadership

Leadership is a very important thing in groups, but not to use their powers against their team members. A group enviroment is not cause for overpowering another. It is all about working together, and accepting your idea may not be the greatest. Leadership isn't about command or control, fear or force, demand or denial. It's about respect and responsibility.

Teamwork

Setting up teams are a effective way to accomplish goals. However, the only way teams can reach it's full potential for greatness, is if everyone is doing what they are supposed to be doing to help the team. Otherwise, even one person slacking off could cause a lot of unnecessary stress on the others.

It also takes an open mind. There is a very, very low chance of a group unanimously agreeing on many things. So each of the present people there will to be able to have a rational discussion about different ideas instead of simply ignoring it.
